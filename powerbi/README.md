# 📊 Power BI Dashboard

## 🎯 Objective

To visualize attrition patterns and provide clear business insights for decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### Page 1 — Overview

* KPI cards (Total Employees, Attrition Rate)
* Summary insights

---

### Page 2 — Who is Leaving

* Segmentation by:

  * Age
  * Income
  * Tenure
* Identification of high-risk employee groups

---

### Page 3 — Where are They Leaving

* Attrition by:

  * Job Role
  * Department
* Highlights organizational risk areas

---

### Page 4 — Why Employees Leave

* Overtime impact (main driver)
* Job satisfaction analysis
* Work-life balance trends

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

Overtime is the strongest driver of attrition, with employees working overtime being significantly more likely to leave.
